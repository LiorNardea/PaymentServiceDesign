Reconciliation Lambda — EventBridge cron, every 5 min · not an always-running service
  • SELECT processing > 10 min
  • stripe.paymentIntents.retrieve(stripe_pi_id)
  • UPDATE final status
  • Publish PaymentStateChanged to SNS (fallback publisher)
  • Auto-cancel after 24h max-age
Each subscriber filters by consumerId — ignores events belonging to other services.
SNS/SQS is at-least-once: subscribers must process events idempotently.
The Reconciliation Lambda is the fallback publisher when the Stripe webhook is missed.
